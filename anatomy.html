Anatomy Vocabulary

Learn the essential words for anatomy, physiology, and medical sciences.

🦴 Skeletal System

Skull

Bony structure protecting brain.

Spine

Backbone made of vertebrae.

Ribcage

Protects chest organs.

Femur

Thigh bone, longest in body.

Scapula

Shoulder blade.

Clavicle

Collarbone.

Pelvis

Bony structure supporting spine & legs.

Tibia

Shin bone.

Mandible

Lower jawbone.

Patella

Kneecap.

Ulna

Inner forearm bone.

Radius

Outer forearm bone.

Phalanges

Finger and toe bones.

Metatarsals

Bones in the mid-foot.

Sacrum

Triangular bone at the base of the spine.

Coccyx

Tailbone.

🧠 Nervous System

Brain

Controls body functions.

Neuron

Nerve cell transmitting signals.

Spinal Cord

Signal pathway between brain & body.

Cerebellum

Controls balance and coordination.

Synapse

Junction between neurons.

Cerebrum

Largest brain part, controls thinking and memory.

Medulla Oblongata

Controls breathing and heartbeat.

Axon

Part of neuron carrying signals away.

Dendrite

Branches of neuron receiving signals.

Reflex Arc

Pathway for automatic responses.

Myelin Sheath

Protective covering around nerves.

Peripheral Nerves

Nerves outside brain and spinal cord.

❤️ Circulatory System

Heart

Pumps blood throughout body.

Aorta

Main artery from heart.

Vein

Carries blood back to heart.

Capillary

Smallest blood vessels.

Artery

Carries oxygenated blood.

Ventricle

Lower chambers of heart.

Atrium

Upper chambers of heart.

Pulmonary Artery

Carries blood from heart to lungs.

Pulmonary Vein

Brings oxygenated blood to heart.

Platelets

Help clot blood.

Red Blood Cells (RBCs)

Carry oxygen.

White Blood Cells (WBCs)

Fight infections.

Plasma

Liquid part of blood.

💨 Respiratory System

Lungs

Gas exchange organs.

Trachea

Windpipe.

Bronchi

Air passageways.

Alveoli

Tiny sacs for gas exchange.

Nasal Cavity

Filters and warms air.

Diaphragm

Muscle for breathing.

Pharynx

Throat passageway for air and food.

Larynx

Voice box.

Pleura

Membrane covering lungs.

Cilia

Tiny hairs trapping dust in airways.

Epiglottis

Flap preventing food entering windpipe.

💪 Muscular System

Biceps

Arm flexion muscle.

Triceps

Arm extension muscle.

Quadriceps

Front thigh muscles.

Hamstrings

Back thigh muscles.

Deltoid

Shoulder muscle.

Gluteus Maximus

Buttock muscle.

Pectorals

Chest muscles.

Calf (Gastrocnemius)

Lower leg muscle.

Abdominals

Stomach muscles.

Obliques

Side abdominal muscles.

Latissimus Dorsi

Large back muscles.

Sternocleidomastoid

Neck muscle.

🍽️ Digestive System

Stomach

Food digestion organ.

Liver

Processes nutrients.

Intestines

Absorb nutrients.

Pancreas

Produces enzymes & insulin.

Esophagus

Tube carrying food to stomach.

Gallbladder

Stores bile.

Appendix

Small pouch attached to intestine.

Salivary Glands

Produce saliva.

Mouth

Entry for food, chewing begins digestion.

Teeth

Crush and grind food.

Rectum

Stores waste.

Anus

Exit for waste.

Duodenum

First part of small intestine.
