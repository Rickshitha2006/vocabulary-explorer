📘 What is Vocabulary?

Vocabulary is the collection of words a person knows and uses to communicate effectively. A strong vocabulary allows you to express ideas clearly, understand complex texts, and improve professional and social communication.

🌟 Why is Vocabulary Important?

Communication

Express ideas clearly and confidently in any situation.

Reading & Writing

Understand texts easily and write more persuasively.

Career & Exams

Boost confidence for presentations, interviews, and exams.

💡 Tips to Improve Vocabulary

  • Maintain a personal word journal and review it daily.
  • Read books, articles, and newspapers to learn words in context.
  • Listen to podcasts and audiobooks to hear words in use.
  • Practice conversations using new vocabulary.
  • Play vocabulary games and quizzes for fun learning.
